Oven

29,990
For ovens, whether microwave or conventional, typical problems include heating element failures that lead to uneven cooking, door seal issues that cause heat loss, and control board malfunctions, which affect temperature regulation. In microwaves, a faulty magnetron can result in the appliance not heating. Regular cleaning and ensuring the door seals properly are key to keeping ovens in good working condition.

Washing Machine
35,000
Washing machines are essential for cleaning clothes. Common issues include the drum not spinning, water leakage due to worn hoses, and drainage problems from clogged pumps. Regular maintenance includes cleaning the lint filter and checking the hoses for wear. Many washing machines are designed with large drum capacities, making them ideal for families. Innovations in washing technology, such as inverter motors, ensure quieter operation and longer-lasting performance, making laundry less of a chore.
Vacuum Cleaner
10,500
Vacuum cleaners are essential for maintaining cleanliness. Issues often include clogged filters, loss of suction, or motor failure. Regularly cleaning filters and checking for blockages can improve their efficiency. Some vacuum cleaners are equipped with attachments that make cleaning tight spaces, curtains, and furniture easier. With the latest technology, many models also offer bagless designs and energy efficiency, catering to eco-conscious users.
